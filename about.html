Activa Inc. is committed to the research & development of LED lighting products. Based on the research groups` efforts, SYM BANG has innovated and manufacturing the more efficient LED lighting products，especially in the indoor lighting products.

Our management's commitment to total quality control (TQC) makes SYM BANG's LED lighting products have highly reliable and competitive. New products are invented regularly. We are available for special design requirements and modifications of our existing product line.
